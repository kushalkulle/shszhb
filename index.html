Welcome to the official website of Sanghamitra High School, Zaheerabad, Medak District, Telangana, India. We have made every endeavor to address all the requirements of our students, parents and staff.

Sanghamitra High School was established in the year 1994 by late Sri S.Jaipal Reddy (founder). It was a humble beginning with just 114 students and 7 teachers in a rented building. I feel very proud that we have our own spacious building in 2 Acre campus. Our family tree has grown to over 1000 children and 70 teaching and non-teaching staff. Parents and community are an integral part of the educational institution system. The school management, parents, community and even students are all stake holders. The involvement of each and every segment of the system is important for the success of the educational institution.
