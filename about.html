Philosophy

It is universally believed that the foremost aim of education is all round development of the child. The end of education is man making. Education is that which forms the character, increases the strength of mind, expands the Intellect and enables one to stand on one's feet. Established in 1994 Sanghamitra High School owes its origin to Sanghamitra Educational Society (An educational Society registered with Registrar of Societies) Hyderabad vide Registration No.2392/95 has been incorporated with the main object of establishing institutions to impart education and training, especially to lift self-confidence, creative thinking and entrepreneurship. The philosophy is simple, quality education should be made affordable for every section of society. Quality education does not just mean academics, but the entire gamut of extra-curricular and co-curricular activities. There are absolutely no hidden costs, nor funds collected to maintain equality among all our students. We do not organize expensive excursions and trips, nor run a canteen and stationery in our school premises or encourage students to celebrate their birthdays lavishly in the school. For us each and every Sanghamitra student is special, irrespective of economic background, caste, creed, sex and religion.

Motto

Our school motto is “ Save Dharma, Dharma Saves You “ .In other words ,if we follow the path of virtue in whatever we do , all other things will be taken care of . In fact , our school’s vision and mission statements are woven around the motto.

Mission Statement

We put in to Practice UNESCO’S suggestion of education for the 21st century by using the four pillars of Learing to know Learing to do Learing to live together Learing to be Enable students to attain a high level of proficiency in their academics. To foster eternal values of love, compassion, tolerance, self control, truthfulness, honesty and service. Develop them to become responsible citizens with secular, national and international outlook. To involve them in social activities and instill in them a spirit of service and help them to evolve as one of the best students and global citizens.

Vision Statement

We aim to develop the intellectual, aesthetic, emotional, moral and spiritual dimensions of students to shape them into holistic individuals with right temperament and capabilities. Our vision is to provide quality education to make learning a pleasurable experience and help to train them to play a positive role in the community development with the right temperament and capabilities.

Parents Role

In fulfilling the vision and mission of the school, parents have a great role to play. They have to re-inforce those values. It is said that, to educate a child, parents should educate themselves. It is only through example, role model, principles and values can be transmitted effectively. The learning process of a child starts from mata, pita and then guru comes into the picture. I would like to request parents to spend at least an hour with their children and help in the renewal of different facts, physical, intellectual and emotional needs of the children. Parents should spend at least 15 to 30 minutes of exercising with their children either by walking or aerobics or any other physical activity, which helps them to keep fit. Parents should not look at the marks in the progress report but assess the child’s development through behavioral changes. Give your children catalystic and supportive facilitation rather than discourage and demotivate them when they get less marks than your expectation. Children are put to enormous stress and strain in today’s competitive world. As a parent try to help your children by giving them moral support and boost up their courage.
