HI, WE’RE CREO

COMPANY

Creo was born in 2010 in Bangalore, India with a vision to create a legendary tech company, one that builds products used and loved by millions of people all over the world, and strives to be one of the best places to work.

We loved the opportunity we saw in developing apps: the pace of growth of the market, the difficult business and technological problems that favor those few that are smart enough to solve them, the infinite variety of use cases, the intimate relationship of consumers with their phones. Through a lot of incredibly hard work, smarts, creativity and failures we created some popular apps. In a market where most people had an amateurish approach, we believed in being ultra-structured, technologically advanced, and analytical.

The next frontier for us is to become one of the greatest companies in the mobile app market, and then apply all that we’ve learnt to other industries, in our everlasting quest to build and extraordinary company. If our journey forward sounds exciting to you, don't hesitate to get in touch. We’d love to talk to you.

VALUES

Creo is founded on values we all share and are ready to stand for, and that bring us together well beyond the products we’ve made. They’ve defined our identity since the beginning, and will continue to do so, no matter how our business will evolve.

  1. We are nerds

    We’re obsessed with making products that are widely used, loved, and profitable. We believe in true knowledge and creative experimentation. We truly enjoy thinking out of the box, and destroying rules and best practices, when they get in the way of innovation.

  2. Our work matters

    We’re business minded and enjoy working on products lots of people actually love using: thats the surest way to profits we know. For us it’s exceptionally rewarding to know our creations end up in the hands of millions of people all around the world, and that our work matters to them.

  3. We seek the truth

    We praise rationality and we value real insight. We’re very data-driven and we’re skeptical of commonly-held beliefs. We believe in knowledge and relentlessly foster learning and experimentation. Here, you’re going to learn at a pace faster than you ever thought possible.

  4. We are ambitious

    We believe in personal initiative and make sure every voice is heard. Tough challenges and difficult problems are exciting to us, and we see them as a means to reaching our full potential.

  5. We are entrepreneurial

    We have a deeply-ingrained culture of independence and ownership. Shape the projects you’re assigned to as if they were yours, bring forth your ideas, and help steer the company. At Creo you’re expected to make a difference, and your opinions and ideas truly matter.

  6. We embrace challenge

    We challenge each other to find smarter solutions, think outside the box, and always raise the bar. You won’t be successful here, unless you accept that great feats are hard to achieve, and you’re all about going the extra mile, and pushing beyond your limits.

  7. We are transparent

    We trust each other and leave complete freedom to work how and when we like. We’re so transparent that you’ll have complete access from the get-go, and we encourage you to become a leader.

  8. We are free

    We believe in honesty and trust, and enforce no rules. We reject the ideas of control and imposition. Work how you are most comfortable, nobody is going to check on you. You’ll only be judged based on your contribution and value to the team.

  9. We are a tribe

    We’re a young, cohesive team, and we put our hearts and souls in our profession and our goals. We love spending time together both within and outside of work, and forging lifelong bonds. We share the pain and help each other out generously.

  10. We support each other

    We’ll always pick team over individual, and love the saying “one for all, and all for one”. We praise generosity and we all take our share of the (few) painful, boring tasks. We’re there for each other when the shit hits the fan, even outside of work.
